About Us


Established in the year 2000, Anub Furniture began as a small endeavor but has steadily grown over time. Today, we proudly operate three distinct factory setups:


A 7500 Sqft Factory for Woodworking

A 2500 Sqft Factory for Manufacturing Modular Partition Systems

A 1000 Sqft Powder Coating Plant

Our primary focus lies in the manufacturing of Modular Furniture Systems, encompassing both Panel-Based and Desk-Based solutions. We serve as consultants to our clients, offering comprehensive solutions tailored to their specific needs. Our in-house team includes designers, production drawing draftsmen, skilled and unskilled labor, and, most importantly, senior management with over 30 years of combined experience. The company maintains strict quality control standards, ensuring the delivery of impeccable products.
